Water's Beacon

Official Hazard Authorities & Contacts

A verified directory of agencies for real-time, accurate updates on ocean hazards. Follow these sources to stay informed and safe.

Important Safety Notice

This is a resource directory. In an immediate, life-threatening emergency, always contact your local emergency services first (e.g., 112, 108, or your local Coast Guard).

National & International Agencies

INCOIS

The Indian National Centre for Ocean Information Services provides early warnings on tsunamis, storm surges, and other marine hazards for India.

  • Focus: Tsunami Warnings, Ocean State Forecast, Storm Surges
www.incois.gov.in @ESSO_INCOIS

India Meteorological Dept.

The principal government agency for meteorology and the primary source for cyclone and severe weather warnings in India.

  • Focus: Cyclone Warnings, Weather Forecasts, Seismology
mausam.imd.gov.in @Indiametdept

NOAA (USA)

A leading global authority on weather, oceans, and climate. Their hurricane and tsunami warnings are monitored worldwide.

  • Focus: Hurricane Forecasting, Tsunami Warnings, Marine Weather
www.noaa.gov @NOAA

Regional & Hazard-Specific Authorities

Indian Coast Guard

The principal agency for at-sea emergencies, maritime law enforcement, and search and rescue operations in Indian waters.

  • Focus: Search & Rescue, Oil Spills, Maritime Safety
  • Emergency Hotline: 1554
indiancoastguard.gov.in

State Disaster Management

Coordinates disaster response at the state level. Follow your local coastal state's DMA for evacuation orders and shelter information.

  • Focus: Evacuation, Shelters, Local Warnings (e.g., OSDMA for Odisha)
Find Your State's Website

Marine Animal Stranding

If you find a stranded, injured, or deceased marine animal (whale, dolphin, turtle), do not approach. Report it immediately.

  • Primary Contact: State Forest Department (Wildlife/Marine Division)
